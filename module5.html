Lierature and Foundational Skills


flickr photo shared by FatMandy under a Creative Commons ( BY-NC-ND ) license

In this module we will examine the foundational skills required in the Common Core State Standards and connect these skills to literature or children's writing.

  • What are the Foundational skills required in the CCSS?
  • How can we connect children's literature and explicit instruction in foundations?
  • What are some great stories to share?

Through reading, writing , and particapting as a class we will explore these questions.
