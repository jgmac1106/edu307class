Picture Books

Intro text: 1-2 lines: In this module we will learn and explore the aspects and elements of picture books and why they are important in the elementary literature curriculum.

  • Why is it beneficial to teach children using picture books?
  • What are the components of a picture book?
  • What are the different ways to utilize picture books in the elementary school classroom?

Through reading, writing , and particapting as a class we will explore these questions.

The Readings

Adrianne P Hunt & Janet ReuterReadability and Children's Picture Books

Peggy Agostino SharpTeaching with Picture Books throughout the Curriculum

The Tasks

Read

Complete the readings.

Annotate one of the readings.

Write

After reading several of Aesop’s fables, try your hand at writing a fable of your own, using animal characters in the starring roles and leaving a moral to be learned at the end.

In the article Teaching with Picture Books throughout the Curriculum written by Peggy Agostino Sharp, why does Sharp think picture books can be used in various subjects? Provide examples to support your thoughts.

Participate

Choose a favorite children’s picture book and create a video reading the book and talk about why you like the story.
