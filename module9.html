Mythology

In this module we will explore the use of mythology in the classroom. Myhtologies tell gives us a window into the past and a peak at our present.

  • How is culture reflected in the stories we tell?
  • Are myths useful in a modern classroom?
  • Why do these stories endure?

Through reading, writing , and particapting as a class we will explore these questions.
